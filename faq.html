Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ShroomBloom

How long does it take for mushrooms to grow? ▼

Most mushroom varieties from ShroomBloom kits typically complete their first flush in 7-14 days from the time you receive the kit. The exact timeline depends on the strain, environmental conditions (temperature, humidity), and how well you follow the care instructions in your video guide.

What temperature should I maintain? ▼

Most mushroom strains thrive in temperatures between 55-75°F (13-24°C). Optimal temperature varies by strain, which is why our Grow App tracks strain-specific requirements. Keep your kit away from direct sunlight and extreme heat or cold for best results.

How much humidity does my kit need? ▼

Humidity levels should be maintained around 80-95% for optimal mushroom growth. This is why we recommend misting your kit 2-3 times daily or placing it in a humid environment. The humidity monitoring features in your Grow App help you track this precisely.

Can I get multiple flushes from one kit? ▼

Yes! Most ShroomBloom kits will produce 2-4 flushes depending on the strain and how well you maintain the kit's conditions. After harvesting, allow 7-10 days for the kit to rest, then initiate the next flush by repeating the misting cycle. Check your Grow App for strain-specific flush schedules.

What should I do if my mushrooms aren't growing? ▼

If growth has stalled, check these factors: temperature (is it in the optimal range?), humidity (are you misting 2-3 times daily?), and light (indirect light is fine). Common issues include under-misting, temperatures too cold, or inadequate air exchange. Our Growing Community on Discord can help troubleshoot!

How do I store my mushrooms after harvesting? ▼

Fresh mushrooms should be stored in the refrigerator in a paper bag or breathable container for up to 7-10 days. Never store in plastic bags, as moisture can cause spoilage. For longer storage, you can dry your mushrooms using a food dehydrator or oven on low heat, then store them in an airtight container.
