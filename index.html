Stage Tips
🌡️
Colonization · Day 1–21
Ideal Temp: 75–80°F
Keep your kit in a warm, dark spot. Avoid direct sunlight. A seedling heat mat set to low works great.
💧
Colonization · Day 1–21
Don't Open Yet
Resist the urge to open the bag during colonization. The sealed environment is critical for contamination prevention.
⚠️
Watch For · Any Stage
Signs of Contamination
Green, black, or orange patches = mold. Pink or red discoloration = bacterial contamination. Act immediately.
📍
Pinning · Day 21–28
Initiate Fruiting
Once 70%+ colonized, introduce fresh air and indirect light to trigger pinning. Humidity is everything now.
🌿
